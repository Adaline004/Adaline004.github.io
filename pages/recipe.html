Carrot Juice Recipe

The Carrot Vitamin Vitality

INGREDIENTS

  1. 3 large carrots, halved
  2. 2 passion fruits, seeds scooped out
  3. 1/2 inch piece of ginger root
  4. 2 apples

PREPARATION

  1. Feed the carrots, then the passion fruit seeds, followed by the ginger root and then the apples.

  2. Pour over ice and enjoy the tangy goodness.
